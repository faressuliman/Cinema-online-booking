Synopsis

In the Hundred Acre Wood, the once peaceful domain of Winnie the Pooh and his friends, a mysterious force threatens to consume everything in darkness. To save their home, they must embark on a perilous journey through treacherous terrain, encountering strange creatures and facing their deepest fears.
